Text & Reasoning (Large Language Models)

  • GPT-5.2 (OpenAI): The heavy-hitter for complex reasoning, data analysis, and general-purpose chat.
  • Claude Opus 4.5 (Anthropic): Known for its "human-like" writing style and exceptional safety/ethics guardrails.
