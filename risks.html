Risks & Challenges of Generative AI

1. Hallucinations

AI predicts text based on probability, not truth. This can lead to confident but false answers.

Example: Fake legal citations that sound real.

2. Data Privacy & Leakage

Public AI tools can ingest sensitive data. The Samsung leak (2023) showed how internal code became exposed.

3. Bias & Fairness

AI inherits human bias from training data, affecting hiring, lending, and decision systems.

4. Intellectual Property (IP)

Unclear ownership of AI-generated content and training data usage.

5. Cybersecurity & Deepfakes

  • Prompt Injection: Tricks AI into bypassing safety.
  • Deepfakes: Realistic fake audio/video for fraud.
